Toddlers

Toddler program is ages 18 months-4.5 years. **If you child is under the age of 2 you will be required to be out on the floor with you child. These classes are fun filled and all about learning! Your children will be learning in a safe and loving environment that is JUDGMENT FREE! Your toddler will have 30 minutes of class, which includes: 10 minutes of warm up games and stretching and 20 minutes of structured rotation! The structured rotations include: bars, beams, tumbling, climbing, fine motor skills, and hand eye coordination.

Kids

Kids program is for ages 4.5-6.5 years of age. These classes are fun filled and all about improvement! Your child will be encouraged to always try their best and strive to become better during every class. Your child will have 45 minutes of class, which includes: 10 minutes of warm up games and stretching and 35 minutes of structured rotations! Structured rotations include: vault, bars, beam, and tumbling.

Recreational

Recreational program is for ages 6.5+ These classes are fun based and all about success! Your child will be encouraged to succeed in everything they do, whether that takes a day or a year! Your child will be encouraged to always take another step forward no matter how scary it may seem. Your child will have 60 minutes of class, which includes: 15 minutes of warm ups and stretching and 45 minutes of structured rotations and stations. Rotations and stations include: vault, bars, beam, and tumbling.
